Our Mission

While the gender imbalance across America is improving steadily, women in STEM is still limited. This conference is designed for the Horace Mann School to contribute to the larger effort to change this imbalance. By featuring women who are leaders in STEM fields, we hope to inspire those young women who are interested in STEM to pursue their passion, providing greater promise for a future characterized by gender equality in STEM.

Meet Our Keynote Speakers

Jelena Kovačević
Dean of NYU Tandon Engineering School

Jelena Kovačević is the Dean of NYU’s Tandon School of Engineering, the first woman to head the school in its 166-year history.

Leanne Caret
CEO of Boeing Defense, Space, and Security

Leanne Caret is president and CEO of Boeing Defense, Space & Security (BDS), a $26 billion unit that employs over 50,000 STEM professionals.
